Project Updates

Proposal submitted to property owner (CRG)

November 1, 2025

We’ve submitted our formal proposal to CRG, the property owner at Traverse Mountain. This marks the first step toward securing the site for our premium DC fast-charging hub.

We anticipate preliminary feedback in the coming weeks and will update you on any developments.

Tesla NDA executed—program options in review

October 25, 2025

We’ve executed a non‑disclosure agreement with Tesla to explore participation in their charging partner program. We are now reviewing available options.

Our goal is to ensure compatibility with all EVs while leveraging Tesla’s network expertise.

UDOT NEVI clarification: treating Traverse as private/partnership site

October 15, 2025

The Utah Department of Transportation clarified that NEVI funding currently focuses on publicly owned or partnership sites. Our Traverse Mountain hub will be treated as a private / partnership site, shaping our funding strategy accordingly.

We continue to evaluate grants and incentives to deliver best-in-class charging infrastructure.
