The Road Ahead

We're building more than charging stations. We're creating destinations. Places where technology meets humanity. Where every stop powers not just vehicles, but the future of sustainable travel.

Foundation (2025–2030)

It starts in Lehi. Our flagship hub rises with universal fast charging—NACS and CCS, no compromises. This isn't just infrastructure. It's a statement.

Within four years, profitability. Not through shortcuts, but through precision: high-traffic retail partnerships, disciplined operations, and amenities that matter. Fresh food. Workspace. Safe, brilliantly lit environments where families feel secure at any hour.

From there, we expand across the Wasatch Front—Provo, Orem, South Jordan. Each location designed to become a preferred stop, not a necessary one. We're building brand loyalty the hard way: by earning it, every single charge.

Scale (2030–2035)

The network extends. Utah to Idaho. Nevada along I-15 and I-84. But we're not just building more—we're building smarter.

Solar canopies and onsite battery storage cut demand peaks, improve uptime, reduce costs. Energy independence at every hub. We launch our own retail concepts: SEI-branded experiences that turn wait time into welcome time.

Fleet programs arrive. Rideshare drivers. Delivery logistics. Dedicated bays, streamlined billing. We're becoming essential infrastructure for the commercial EV revolution.

Leadership (2035–2040)

We move into Colorado. Arizona. New Mexico. Each market brings new partnerships with cities and municipalities who see what we see: charging infrastructure as community infrastructure.

Autonomous vehicles are coming. Smart grids are evolving. We're ready. Our hubs integrate seamlessly with tomorrow's technology because we're building for tomorrow, today.

Recognition follows execution. We become the Mountain West's benchmark for safe, reliable, experience-first EV infrastructure. And we give back: the SEI Foundation launches to support clean energy education and workforce training. Building the future means investing in the people who'll shape it.

Vision (2040–2045)

Coast to coast. A nationwide network of destination hubs. We've become synonymous with reliability, safety, and exceptional guest experience. When drivers think premium charging, they think Sterling.

We help write the standards. Shape best practices. Expand into Canada and Mexico. Our infrastructure crosses borders because sustainable transportation knows no boundaries.

And through it all, we deliver returns—durable, long-horizon value for investors and communities alike. Not quarterly thinking. Generational building.

Technical excellence: In Lehi, we coordinate with Lehi Power for seamless interconnection and service planning. Because great infrastructure requires great partnerships.
