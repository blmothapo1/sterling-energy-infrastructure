About SEI & Traverse

Sterling Energy Infrastructure (SEI) develops premium electric‑vehicle charging destinations. Our Traverse Mountain project will deliver a dependable, high‑power charging experience along I‑15 in Lehi, Utah, featuring a well‑lit canopy and 10‑12 high‑power stalls designed for ≥98% uptime.

By creating a comfortable, convenient place to charge, we aim to recapture lost demand for surrounding businesses and keep drivers on the property for 20‑45 minutes, benefitting travelers and local partners alike.
