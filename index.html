Our Mission

Sterling Energy Infrastructure (SEI) is building a new standard for EV charging infrastructure—one that puts reliability, safety, and the driver experience first.

We're developing premium DC fast-charging hubs across Utah that combine high-power technology with thoughtful design, strategic location, and unwavering uptime commitments.

Why Choose SEI?

≥98% Uptime Guarantee

Our infrastructure is built for reliability. Every site is engineered and monitored to deliver ≥98% uptime, ensuring you can charge when you need to.

Premium Amenities

Well-lit canopies, clean facilities, and strategic proximity to restaurants, retail, and services—making charging time productive time.

Universal Compatibility

Supporting both CCS and NACS (Tesla) standards, our sites welcome all EV drivers with high-power charging stalls.

Strategic Locations

Positioned at key corridors and destinations across Utah, our hubs are designed for convenience and accessibility.

Safe & Secure

Every location features comprehensive lighting, monitored environments, and 24/7 support for peace of mind.

Community Focused

We partner with local property owners and businesses to create charging infrastructure that serves and strengthens communities.
